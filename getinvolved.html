Get Involved

Sign Up As A Tutor!

If you are a Stuyvesant student interested in tutoring middle school students for the SHSAT, join us! We are low-commitment and you will be tutoring a minimum of 40 minutes / week over Zoom for one hour of volunteering credit. You may earn extra hours through grading homework or creating supplementary SHSAT materials. There are opportunities to earn volunteer hours through our partnerships with Stuyvesant Key Club, Red Cross, and Arista, or simply in our club, Stuy Prep. Sign up in the form below.

Sign Up For Our Program!

If you are a middle school administrator / teacher / parent and would like to partner with us, fill out the form below! It is non-binding and we will contact you to provide extra information and coordinate the sign-up process. Our sessions are currently not open to individual students, but let us know if you're interested down below through the interest form.

If you are a student / parent from one of our partner schools, your school administrator will send you an email about our program. Follow the instructions in that email to register for our program.
