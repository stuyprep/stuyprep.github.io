Stuy Prep

Providing Free SHSAT Tutoring to Local Middle Schools

Join Us

Equal Opportunity for All

Low-income students make up 75% of the NYC high school population, but only 40% of those offered a seat at a Specialized High School. We aim to make SHSAT tutoring accessible to families who may not have the resources to spend thousands on test prep.

By Students, for Students

Run by students from Stuyvesant High School, Stuy Prep offers a 6-week intensive virtual SHSAT prep program. Now in our 8th year, we have assisted hundreds of students on their admissions journey. We welcome prospective volunteer tutors to help with teaching and curriculum building.
